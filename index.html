Mission

SheSec Pakistan, (founded by Ifrah Iman and Khaula Karim) is a community to uplift women in cyber security in Pakistan.
Our members include learners as well as women working in different cyber security domains such as red teams, blue teams in renowned companies.

SheSec Pakistan's primary mission is to provide a space for women who are enthusiastic about Information Security and to give them a platform to share resources, conduct webinars & trainings, share information about career oppurtunities in different domains of cyber security through out Pakistan.

About

Shesec Pakistan is a non profit community for Pakistani women where they support, collaborate, learn and share their experiences about latest cyber security trends with each other.

We participate & arrange online events, sessions & workshops covering different cyber security domains such as job hunting, web application hacking, etc
