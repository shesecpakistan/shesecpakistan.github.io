SheSec Pakistan

Information Security community dedicated to Pakistani Women.

About

SheSec Pakistan is a community for Pakistani women where they support, collaborate, learn and share their experiances with latest cyber security trends with each other.

We also participate & arrange online events, sessions & workshops covering different cyber security domains such as job hunting, web application hacking, etc




Mission

SheSec Pakistan, was founded by Ifrah Iman and Khaula Karim is a community to uplift women in cyber security in Pakistan.
Our members consist of learners as well as women working in different cyber security domains such as red teams, blue teams in renowned companies.

SheSec Pakistan's primary mission is to provide a space for women (cis & trans) who are enthusiastic about Information Security and to give them a platform to share resources, conduct webinars & trainings, share information about career oppurtunities in different domains of cyber security through out Pakistan.
